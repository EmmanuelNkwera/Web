  • The Client

    Refersion helps merchants connect with customers at scale through affiliate and influencer marketing. Their leading performance marketing platform manages, tracks, and grows ambassador, influencer, and affiliate programs with first-party tracking, unlimited offers, and custom commission structures for e-commerce brands.

    Refersion was initially developed to support Shopify and Shopify Plus store owners. A few years later, Shopify stores account for 90% of its business. Refersion also supports other popular e-commerce platforms such as Magento, Chargebee, BigCommerce, Salesforce Commerce Cloud, Stripe, and WooCommerce.

    The challenge

    They were looking for a fresh perspective on their outdated website and branding, focusing on an easily-editable CMS that their marketing team could update regularly.

    1.Assessment and Planning:

    Begin by conducting a comprehensive assessment of the current website and branding. Identify what's outdated, what needs improvement, and what aspects are still relevant. Define clear goals for the project, such as enhancing the user experience, modernizing the design, and making the website more user-friendly. Determine the specific requirements for the CMS, considering factors like ease of use, scalability, and compatibility with existing systems.

    2.Choose the Right CMS:

    Research and select a CMS that aligns with the project's goals. Popular choices include WordPress, Drupal, Joomla, and more. Consider factors like the learning curve, customization capabilities, and support options. Ensure the chosen CMS provides user-friendly content editing tools for the marketing team, such as a visual editor, drag-and-drop functionality, and an intuitive interface.

    The solution

    We did a complete rebrand for Refersion with a brand new website, optimized UX, and CMS integration that benefited the marketing and development teams.

    1.Complete Rebranding:


    A rebrand typically involves a strategic overhaul of the company's visual identity, including its logo, color scheme, typography, and overall design aesthetics. This can give the brand a fresh and modern look while maintaining its core values and message.

    2.Brand New Website:

    Developing a brand new website often signifies a departure from the previous online presence. This could mean starting from scratch or rebuilding the existing site with a new architecture and design.
