Graphic Designer




  • JOB TITLE: GRAPHIC DESIGNER

    Company: Piche

    Location: Remote

    Job Type: Full-time

    Job Description: As a Graphic Designer at Piche. you will play a crucial role in shaping the visual identity of our brand. You will work closely with our marketing team to create visually stunning designs that communicate our message effectively and engage our target audience. Your creativity and attention to detail will be key in developing graphics for various digital and print assets. including but not limited to:

    -Marketing materials

    -Social media content

    -Website graphics

    -Website graphics

    -Packaging designs

    -Event collateral

    -Product illustrations

    Responsibilities:

    - Collaborate with the marketing team to understand project goals and objectives.

    -Design and produce visually compelling graphics and layouts for digital and print media.

    -Create and edit images, infographics. and other visual content.

    -Maintain brand consistency across all design projects.

    -Stay up-to-date with industry trends and incorporate them into your designs.

    -Work efficiently to meet project deadlines.

    Qualifications:

    -Bachelor's degree in Graphic Design or related field (or equivalent work experience). - Proven experience as a Graphic Designer. preferably in a similar industry. - Proficient in Adobe Creative Suite (Photoshop, Illustrator, lnDesign). - Strong portfolio showcasing a range of design projects. - Excellent communication and collaboration skills. - Ability to work independently and manage multiple projects simultaneously. - Attention to detail and a passion for creativity.

    Benefits:

    -Competitive salary

    -Health, dental, and vision insurance

    -401(k) retirement plan

    -Paid time off and holidays

    -Creative and collaborative work environment

    -Opportunities for professional development and growth
